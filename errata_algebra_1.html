Component Improvement in Digital Date Updated
Teacher Guide/Student Workbook, Section D, Lesson 10, Practice Problem 7, TG pg. 143/SW pg. 87 The histogram has been updated to show the second bar reaching 9, not 10.  Therefore, the total data points should be 25, not 26.  8/19/2025

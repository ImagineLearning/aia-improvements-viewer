The following improvements have been made in the digital version of the Geometry Imagine IM curriculum and will be reflected in the next edition of the print materials.

Component Improvement in Digital Date Updated
Student Workbook Section A, Lesson 6, pg. 54

In the Lesson Summary, information was added above the diagrams and below the bullet points to match changes in the Teacher Guide. 
"These diagrams illustrate both types of constructions."

 8/19/2025
Student Workbook Section A, Lesson 2 Warm-up, pg. 15 Student workbook has bee updated to match the Teacher Guide labeling of a, b, c, d instead of 1, 2, 3, 4. 9/19/2025
Teacher Guide, Section B, Lesson 10, Activity 2, pg. 148 The Student Task Statement for question 2 is missing "See diagram." 8/19/2025
Student Workbook, Section C, Lesson 16, Practice Problem 7 The fourth image is mislabeled Translation 2, and has been corrected to Translation 4. 8/20/2025
Student Workbook, Section B, Lesson 10, Practice Problem 6, pg. 101 The answer options a, d, and e are incomplete.  They have been updated to a. AD=BD, d. EA+ED, and e. ED=DB 8/20/2025
Teacher Guide, Unit-at-a-Glance, pg. 3 Section D Goals are listed twice, Section E should say "There are no new goals for this section." 8/20/25

 

Component Improvement in Digital Date Updated
Teacher Guide, Section B, Lesson 8, Activity 3, pg. 138, Launch The Launch has been updated.  It now says, "Display one of the correct, complete student-created diagrams from the Cool-down of the Side-Angle-Side Triangle Congruence lesson." 8/20/2025
Teacher Guide, Section B, Lesson 11, pg. 167 Warm-up The Building On standard 7.G.B.8 has been replaced with G-CO.B.8 8/20/2025
Teacher Guide, Section B, Lesson 10, pg. 151, Lesson Overview The timeline incorrectly shows the Warm-up as 5 minutes, when it should be 10 minutes. 8/20/2025

Teacher Guide, Section A, Lesson 3, pg. 45, Warm-up In the Student Task Statement, the 2nd bullet point has been revised to: Segment ABAB is longer than segment A′B′A′B′. 8/20/2025
Teacher Guide, Section A, Lesson 2, pg. 28, Warm-up In the Student Task Statement question 2, the student response is wrong.  It should say "LYXB isn't the name of the quadrilateral shown. LXYB is one way to name that shape."  8/20/2025
